Eggs Benedict

Description

A fancier way to make breakfast, Eggs Benedict is popular in America. Despite the fancy sounding ingredients, it is not particularly difficult to make if you have some experience. Note: final product may not match picture.

Preparing time: 20 minutes, cooking time: 4 minutes. Serves 4 people.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 4 slices of toast
  • 12 slices of bacon
  • Salad greens

Hollandaise sauce ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 150 grams of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of white vine vinegar
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Fill the boiling pot halfway with water and let it boil. Turn the heat down to minimum.
  2. Melt the butter in a separate pan and take it off the heat.
  3. Take a heat-resistant bowl, place the egg yolks there and place the bowl in the water-filled pot.
  4. Ensure that the water is boiling.
  5. Whisk the eggs and when it starts to thicken, add white vine vinegar.
  6. Slowly add the melted butter while whisking.
  7. Remove the sauce from the pot and add salt and pepper.
  8. Add some lemon juice and have a quick taste. Add more lemon juice until you're satisfied with the taste.
  9. Take a wide pot and fill it halfway with water.
  10. Add salt, 1 tablespoon of vinegar and let it boil. Afterwards, reduce the heat.
  11. Crack all four eggs into separate small bowls.
  12. Stir the water slowly and simultaneously add the yolks.
  13. Let it boil for 2-4 minutes, then take the yolks and place them onto cooking paper.
  14. Fry the bacon and toast the bread.
  15. Place the bacon, poached egg and hollandaise sauce on the toast.
