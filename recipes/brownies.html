Brownies

Description

A classic. Note: final product won't match picture.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of butter
  • 200 grams of dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 200 grams of sugar
  • 100 grams of wheat flour
  • 100 grams of chopped walnuts
  • 0.5 teaspoons of salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Cover the baking tray with cooking paper and heat the oven to 170 degrees Celsius.
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate.
  3. Whisk the eggs and sugar in a blender until foamy.
  4. Add the mixture of melted butter and chocolate and mix thoroughly.
  5. Add flour, salt, chopped walnuts and vanilla extract, mix thoroughly.
  6. Pour the dough into the tray and bake it in 170 degrees Celsius for 30-35 minutes, until the brownies look dry at the top.
  7. Let it cool a bit, then take it out of the tray and slice it in pieces.
